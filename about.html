Welcome to Clearways

Clearways Uganda is a Community Based Organization non profit, non-governmental and non-political organization, ideologically founded in 2016 and registered in 2017 with Soroti municipal council and located in kichinjaji., aliabu cell soroti city.

Clearways Uganda is a Community Based Organization determined to strive and actively engage in all aspects of social and economic transformation in rural and poor urban communities.

The organization was established after realizing high level of school drop outs, increase in numbers of teenage mothers and domestic violence as a result of poverty related to climatic changes, therefore there was need to bridge the gap through provision of vocational life skills and sensitization and advocacy.

Clearways Uganda employs qualified and innovative individuals who have the necessary skills and years of experience to respond to today’s challenging subsistence communities.
