    Who we are.

    Clearways Uganda is a Community Based Organization non profit, non-governmental and non-political organization, ideologically founded in 2016 and registered in 2017 with Soroti municipal council and located in kichinjaji., aliabu cell soroti city. Clearways Uganda is a Community Based Organization determined to strive and actively engage in all aspects of social and economic transformation in rural and poor urban communities.
